F.A.Q

What types of training programs do you offer?

We offer a wide range of programs including Vendor Certification Training, Industrial Attachments, Career Starter Courses, Corporate Upskilling, Project-Based Learning, and Tech Bootcamps.

Do your training programs include certifications?

Yes, many of our programs prepare you for industry certifications like Cisco (CCNA), Microsoft, AWS, CompTIA, and more. You'll receive a certificate of completion as well.

Are your courses beginner-friendly?

Absolutely. We have foundational programs specifically designed for beginners, students, and career switchers, with no prior technical background required.

Do you offer online and in-person training?

Yes, we offer both flexible online classes and instructor-led in-person sessions, depending on the course and your location.

Can I get job assistance after completing a course?

Yes, we provide career support including resume building, interview preparation, internship placement, and job referrals through our partner network.

How do I enroll in a course at Intrex Digital Learning Hub?

You can enroll by visiting our website, contacting us via email or phone, or visiting our training center. Our team will guide you through the registration process.
